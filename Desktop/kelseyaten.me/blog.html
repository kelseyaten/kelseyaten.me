Rocky Mountain National Park, Colorado
June 13, 2017

With a quick pit stop in Estes Park, Mom, Dad and I made our way up Ridge Trail Road through Rocky National Park. Ridge trail road is part of Highway 34, the longest paved road in the United States. We climbed ridge trail road for 23 miles into the Rockies to an elevation of 12,183 feet.

The adventure began in the small town of Estes Park. Here we made our way up a mountain overlooking the town by a three minute ride in a tram. The tramway took us to an elevation of 8,707 feet overlooking Estes Park.

Estes Park Tramway

Looking down from the top of the tram you can clearly see the hotel from the Shinning.
